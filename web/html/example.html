About Me

I am a Software Engineer, DevOps practitioner, and Linux systems administrator with a focus on building robust, scalable backend systems. Primarily I write Python and bash, with additional experience in Ruby, Go, and JavaScript.

Over the years, I have designed and implemented automation solutions that reduce downtime, streamline developer workflows, and improve system reliability. My projects include creating automated datacenter failover tools, building CI/CD pipelines for distributed teams, and developing SAML/SSO integrations for secure user lifecycle management. I have also led efforts to modernize and stabilize 100+ legacy microservices, built libraries for generating PII-compliant test data, and automated cross-team task management processes.

My focus is aimed towards operational efficiency, security, and long-term maintainability.
